    • The New York City Subway

    With so many sights around the city to see, you'll have to do quite a bit of traveling. If you rely on walking you certainly won't get to see everything and you probably won't last more than a day. Taxis and Uber/Lyft work well from the airport or in specific situations, but I recommend utilizing the subway. For one, it's an experience that you have to try when you go to New York. Secondly, its cheap considering you'll be using it a lot, and it's a quick way to go from destination to destination. And last but surely not least, the subway is itself an attraction. Artwork and performers can be found at every stop.
